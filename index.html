My name is Bianca and I was born and raised in southern California. I enjoy going on spontaneous foodie adventures and spending quality time with my friends and family. I also love traveling and my goal is to visit as many different countries as I can. I grew up as an only child and was more quiet and reserved, so I tried to diversify myself in high school and join clubs that interested me. However, since the amount clubs offered were still quite limiting, I plan on getting more involved in college and joining more clubs that interest me. I love meeting new people and making new friends, and so I'm recently more seriously considering the prospect of joining a sorority.

I have not had much work experience but my first and only job thus far is working as a waitress in a shabu shabu restaurant. I had the opportunity to work at a place that serves one of my favorite foods and getting to learn how to work with others at the same time. Working is a new experience for me still, but I really enjoy being busy and having a welcoming environment to work in. I also enjoy how fast paced a restaurant is since I feel more comfortable keeping myself occupied rather than having too much free time to spare. I am very intrigued by how a business functions and can't see myself working in another field. My dream job would be working in the hospitality industry managing the business aspects.
